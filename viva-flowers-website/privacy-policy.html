Privacy Policy for BLOCK LAND

Last Updated: May 2024

1. Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in BLOCK LAND. This app is designed to be fully offline, ensuring your data remains on your device.

2. Information Collection

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (such as your name, email address, or phone number).
  • No Data Tracking: We do not use any third-party analytics or tracking tools. Your activity within the app is completely private.
  • Gameplay Data: Statistics, high scores, and game progress are stored locally on your device only.

3. Local Storage

We use local storage (such as SharedPreferences) to save your game progress and settings. This data remains on your device and is never uploaded to our servers or shared with any third parties.

4. Children's Privacy

BLOCK LAND is a casual puzzle game suitable for all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, the app is fully compliant with COPPA and GDPR regulations regarding child privacy. We do not display any advertisements.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your locally stored data. However, as the data is stored on your own device, the security of that data also depends on your device's security settings.

6. Your Rights

You have full control over your data. You can clear all locally stored game data at any time by clearing the app's cache/data in your device settings or by uninstalling the app.

7. Changes to this Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the support links provided on the Google Play Store or at vivaflowersanddecor@gmail.com.
